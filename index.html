What is KommuAssist?

Lane Keep Control

Actively controls steering to keep vehicle centered in lane for enhanced safety and driving convenience.

Adaptive Cruise Control

Maintains a following distance from lead car or cruise at set speed to help relieve fatigue during long hour drives.

Stop and Go

Holds brakes at standstill traffic and auto-departs when lead car moves; relaxes your legs and ease frustrations.




Driven by bukapilot


bukapilot is built upon an open source driving agent named openpilot, which will be supported by Malaysian ourselves. The software enables level 2 autonomous driving functionalities including longitudinal and lateral driver assistance. BukaPilot will be trained upon Malaysia road data; providing a smoother and safer driving experience for everyone in Malaysia.
